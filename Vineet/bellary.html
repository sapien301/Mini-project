About Ballari

Ballari, officially Ballari, in the eponymous Ballari district, is a major city in the state of Karnataka, India. It is 311 km from the state capital of Bengaluru and 358 km from Hyderabad. Ballari has an urban population of 4,10,445 and metro population of 7,70,929 making it one of the most populous cities of Karnataka. Ballari was a part of the first indian commercial flight from Karachi to Madras on 15 October 1932 by JRD Tata.
